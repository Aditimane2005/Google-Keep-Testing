# Bug Report – Google Keep

## 🐞 Bug ID: BUG_01

## 📌 Title:
Settings options (Notes and Lists, Sharing) are not working properly

## 📍 Module:
Settings

## 🔥 Severity:
Medium

## ⚡ Priority:
High

## 🖥 Environment:
- Device: Mobile / Laptop
- OS: Android / Windows
- Browser: Chrome

## 📋 Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Google Keep
2. Go to Settings
3. Enable/Disable options under "Notes and Lists" and "Sharing"
4. Click on Save

## ❌ Actual Result:
Selected settings are not applied or not working as expected.

## ✅ Expected Result:
All selected settings (Notes and Lists, Sharing) should work correctly after saving.
